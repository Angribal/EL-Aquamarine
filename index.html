Welcome to El Aquamarine
El Aquamarine offers incredible views of the Indian Ocean and stunning coastline beauty. Part of the prestigious Relais & Châteaux hotel collection and awarded two Michelin stars for its restaurant by chef AJ Buchanio, El Aquamarine is a unique landmark in the world of classic beach hotels.
A GREAT HOLIDAY IN THE ENVIRONMENT OF LAZUR WATERS

Imagine a secluded island with bright white beaches, a lagoon filled with blue and aquamarine flowers, and coconut trees soaring in the wind. A network of cobbled lanes runs through lush vegetation, connecting rooms with ocean views, gourmet restaurants, sports facilities and an inspiring spa. Our spacious bungalows above water have been carefully designed to provide you with an unforgettable experience during your stay. You can relax in your own pool or in the comfort of your room for a well-deserved vacation. The hotel offers all visitors an unforgettable vacation with elegant rooms, world cuisine, a yacht trip and a luxurious spa. El Aquamarine has four restaurants and five bars. Each of them offers dainty dishes, as well as the widest choice of sake and Japanese whiskey in Maldives.

location
destination El Aquamarine
El Aquamarine Hotel is located in the north of the Maldives, in the unspoiled Lhaviani Atoll. You can get to this piece of tropical paradise by seaplane in the daytime. The flight time is 40 minutes from Male International Airport. The resort is located on a private 400m x 165m coral island surrounded by the Indian Ocean, where the water stays warm all year round, and the shades and color variations on the water surface are amazing and change several times a day.
