El Aquamarine
BAR & restaurant
Culinary excellence meets barefoot luxury, serving a wide variety of gourmet food, from international buffets to fresh seafood and authentic Thai delights. Enjoy a restaurant without borders in an incredible island setting.
Benjarong

A collection of delectable dishes from Europe to Asia, served in two restaurants, three bars, a wine cellar, and private restaurants throughout the island.

Start or end your day with a buffet that spans the globe. Breakfast at Benjarong includes international dishes and a variety of fresh, organic, seasonal fruits, as well as classic pastries. In the evening, choose from sumptuous buffets or delectable à la carte dishes in an unrivaled Maldivian setting.

Ocean Grill

Indulge in a fine dining fusion of delicacies from land and sea. Feast on fresh seafood and choice cuts grilled to perfection, accompanied by side dishes from around the world. The open kitchen adds a dash of drama and the stylish poolside setting is perfect for al fresco lunches or starlit dinners, complemented by wines from the glass cellar.

THE STORY OF THE CHEF
Born in San Sebastian, I studied in Madrid and Zarause before opening my own restaurant in my hometown in 1980, trying to create a new Basque cuisine from one of the centers of creation. America, sharing experiences with hospitality and catering companies. This teaching experience allowed me to spread my "secrets" in television programs and in dozens of recipe books.

I continue to work without rest in order to realize all the projects seething in me. Time will put them in their places, and new projects will open the way.
